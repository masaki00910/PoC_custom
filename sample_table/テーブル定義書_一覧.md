## テーブル関連図（リレーションシップ）

```
ca_code_management (マスタ)
  ├── 1:N → ca_document_selection.ca_document

  ├── 1:N → ca_processmanagement.ca_processtype
  └── 1:N → ca_processmanagement.ca_process_status

ca_document_selection (設定)
  ├── 1:N → ca_ai_prompt.ca_documentid
  ├── 1:N → ca_document_import.ca_document_id
  └── 1:N → ca_ai.ca_document_id

ca_ai_prompt (設定)
  └── 1:N → ca_ai.ca_prompt_id

ca_document_import (コア処理)
  ├── 1:N → ca_document_item.ca_document_id
  ├── 1:N → ca_ai.ca_id
  └── 1:N → ca_inquiry.ca_error_list_management_id

ca_error_list_item_master (マスタ)
  └── 1:N → ca_document_item.ca_field_id

ca_document_item (コア処理)
  ├── 1:N → ca_ai.ca_document_item_id
  └── 1:N → ca_reportprocessingstatus.bpf_ca_document_itemid

systemuser (システム)
  ├── 1:N → ca_document_import.ca_contactperson
  └── 1:N → ca_processmanagement.ca_requester

--- 論理キー結合（証券番号ベース） ---

  └── new_a_policy_number ←→ ca_document_import.ca_policy_number

  └── new_a_policy_number ←→ ca_document_import.ca_policy_number

  └── ca_policy_number ←→ ca_document_import.ca_policy_number

  └── ca_policy_number ←→ ca_document_import.ca_policy_number
```
